FAQs

Order & Payment FAQs

  • How do I place an order?

    To place an order, simply browse our products, add the items you wish to purchase to your cart, and proceed to the checkout page. Follow the on-screen instructions to enter your shipping and payment details.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and direct bank transfers. You can see the full list of accepted methods at the checkout page.

  • Is my payment information secure?

    Yes, your payment information is highly secure. We use industry-standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption to protect your personal and payment details during transmission. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers.

Shipping & Delivery FAQs

  • What are your shipping options?

    We offer several shipping options to meet your needs, including standard shipping, expedited shipping, and in some areas, white-glove delivery for larger furniture items. The available options and their costs will be displayed at checkout.

  • Do you ship internationally?

    Currently, we primarily ship within [mention your primary shipping region/countries]. Please check our shipping policy page or contact our customer support team to inquire about specific international shipping options.

  • How can I track my order?

    Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your delivery.
